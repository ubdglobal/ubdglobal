CEO/Founder

Caroline Usher

Since founding UBD Global in 2010, Caroline Usher has built, from the ground up, a company that is locally and internationally recognised and awarded. Achieving this has taken passion, vision, expertise and knowledge, all of which continues to propel her, UBD Global and their designs forward today.

Caroline Usher's dedication to excellence, innovation and cultural authenticity is just some of what has entrenched her position as an industry-leading figure in the international interior design world. Caroline's ability to continually produce spaces that inspire visual and emotional awe whilst maintaining a subtlety and attention to detail has produced a personal portfolio of successful past projects, prestigious awards and international acclaim rarely seen in the interior design world.

About Us

Who We Are

Founded by Caroline Usher in 2010, UBD Global is a world-renowned, internationally awarded interior design company known for continuously pushing the boundaries of design; not only delivering but elevating client projects above and beyond what was envisioned or expected.

By embracing thinking outside of the box, UBD Global creates designs that continuously exceed clients aspirations. This, partnered with a creativity originating with a background in cinematic set design, produces spaces that evoke emotions, tell stories and inspire, resulting in each project being infused with a unique flair and character that simply isn't found elsewhere.

What We Do

In proven collaboration with esteemed partners Agung and Fariz, Caroline and the team bring a wealth of experience and proven track record not only in the conceptualisation of magnificent Interior design, but in its effective delivery too.

Partner with UBD Global to bring your vision into reality with unparalleled creativity and expertise, forging environments that are not just designed, but experienced.

“We believe every interior should evoke emotion before functionality.”
